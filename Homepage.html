University Website Design

Edinburgh University:

There is a search bar on the top to the left of the page. The colours of the website match the logo of the university. The main menu to select which area of the website you would like to access is across the top. the social media information and contact details are at the bottom of the web page. Other pages and areas of the website can be easily accessed. The important information of the website is written in large text to catch the attention of the reader. The headings are equally distant from one another to keep the website neat and tidy. The colours of the website are not too bright (black. grey and white). There are sections for both students and staff members.

Dundee University:

The menu options can be found across the top of the webpage. The colour scheme of the website also matches the colours of the logo. The search bar to search for a course can be found across the top. Pictures are placed across the site to show the different aspects of the university. There is also detailed information on the different activities that are taking place in the university which is found in a scroll menu. And contact details can be found at the bottom left of the main page.

Glasgow University:

There is video to promote the university. Quick links to other areas of the website to the side which helps the user to get around the website. A search bar is found across the top. News and special events information can be found in the middle of the web page and contact details are found across the bottom of the website. There are also pictures of the university and Glasgow as a city to show people applying what the atmosphere there will be like. Everything is also catalogued in alphabetical order to help the user find what they are searching for.
